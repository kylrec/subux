Year 11 Subject Options Guide

Option Choices

At year 11 students will study six subjects. The following subjects are compulsory:

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science

Students will choose three more subjects.

To attain NCEA Level 1, students will need 80 credits across their five subjects.

Understanding NCEA
Requirements

80+ credits at NCEA levels 1, 2, or 3 Literacy (10 credits from any subject that count towards literacy) Numeracy (10 credits from any subject that count towards numeracy)

Note: the 10 literacy and 10 numeracy credits are included in the 80+ credits needed to achieve NCEA level 1.
